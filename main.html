Objective

Barndominium plans or barn-style house plans feel both timeless and modern. While the term barndominium is often used to refer to a metal building, this collection showcases mostly traditional wood-framed house plans with the rustic look of pole barn house plans. Barn style house plans feature simple, rustic exteriors, perhaps with a gambrel roof or (of course) barn doors. Modern farmhouse style house plans with open floor plans have become very popular
